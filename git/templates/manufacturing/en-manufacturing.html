Stages of organizing a manufacturing business in the UAE

Choosing a working business model
It is necessary to identify markets and territories for business and make sure that the company being created will be able to work without any restrictions. To sell manufactured goods to the local market of the UAE, you need to register a mainland company
Obtaining a preliminary permit for registration of manufacturing
Examination of the requirements that are imposed by departments for the opening of production. Preparation of a project for the Ministry of Economy, which, after approval, issues a preliminary permit and forms a list of necessary approvals for registration of production
Getting additional permissions
Obtaining approvals from the relevant authorities specified in the preliminary permit: the Municipality, Department of Civil Defense, Planning Department, and others that correspond to the profile of industrial or manufacturing activities
Licensing and registration of the company
After obtaining approvals from the relevant departments, you must comply with the legal requirements for registering the selected type of legal entity by registering it at the location of the production being created to confirm the presence of physical address of manufacturing
Certification
It is necessary to pass the certification procedure, which may include laboratory tests for compliance with standards, examination by the Agency for Standardization and Metrology (ESMA), registration with the Municipality, the Ministry of Health, the Department of Civil Defense and other departments
Providing quality support to the company
The final stage will be the selection of a service company that will provide high-quality administrative, legal and financial support, tax accounting, provide support during immigration procedures, bank account opening and registration of foreign employees
