Obtaining prior permission to register production
Preparation of a project summary for the Ministry of Economy, which after studying issues a preliminary permit and forms a list of necessary approvals for registration of production. In some cases additional requirements may apply
Obtaining additional approvals
Obtaining approvals from the relevant departments specified in the preliminary permit: the Municipality, The Department of Civil Defense, the Department of construction, and others that correspond to the profile of production activities
Obtaining a license and company registration
After obtaining approvals from the relevant departments, you must comply with the legal requirements for registering the selected type of legal entity incorporating and placing it at the location of the production being created
Product certification
A process that may include laboratory tests for compliance with standards, examination by the Agency for Standardization and Metrology (ESMA), registration with the Municipality, the Ministry of Health, the Department of Civil Defense, and other agencies

What to consider when starting a business in the UAE

Features of business licensing in
the UAE
Any business activity in
the country is subject to
mandatory licensing on the
local territory or on the
territory of one of the
Free Economic Zones
Registration in the Free Economic
Zone
It gives the right to companies to
conduct business on their territory
and outside the country. FZE of UAE
differs in the types of licenses
and formal business registration
procedures
Registration on the local
territory
Gives the unlimited right to conduct
business in the domestic and foreign
markets of the UAE. The activities of
companies are regulated by the
economic departments of each of
the Emirates
Banking system
The state guarantees investment
protection, free capital entry
and withdrawal, banks operate without
foreign exchange control, but
adhere to KYC standards
Tax system
There are missing major corporate
taxes. When operating on the
domestic market, VAT is paid 5%,
which requires mandatory registration
with the tax authorities and reporting
Immigration procedures
All business owners can
obtain resident visas. It is
possible to freely attract
foreign labor when obtaining
work quotas
Organization of import
All goods must go through the
procedure for obtaining import
permits: provide certificates for
products, obtain approvals from
regulatory authorities
Export and re-export
Tax-free opportunities doing the
international business, powerful
logistics infrastructure, and
the ability to recover previously
paid Value Added Tax (VAT)
Compliance with EU and OECD
When conducting international
activities from the UAE, it is
necessary to confirm the Economic
Substance and the Management
Decision Making Center
