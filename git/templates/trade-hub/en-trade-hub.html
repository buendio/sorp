When starting a trading business in the UAE, you need to check whether your business hypothesis matches the needs of the market and the laws of the country. You also need to evaluate the possibility of obtaining a licence and permits for the import of goods. It is important to choose the most suitable business model for your goals

Stages of opening a trade business in the UAE

Choosing a business model, and confirmation of your business hypothesis
You need to conduct a business analysis of the competitive environment and your business development strategy, since models that work successfully in other countries are often ineffective in the UAE
Choosing geographical coverage of activities
The choice of business registration location depends on the location of your target audience. To supply goods to the domestic market of the UAE, the registration of a local company is required
Choosing the right licence
Any business in the UAE is subject to licencing. It is necessary to analyse the features and additional requirements for obtaining a licence for the supply of goods in the UAE
Obtaining a licence and registration of a company
After obtaining approvals from the competent authorities, it is necessary to fulfil the requirements of the legislation for registration of the selected type of legal entity. You also need to have an approved legal address
Obtaining permission to import
To import goods into the UAE, an import permit is issued. It provides for the certification of a product group
